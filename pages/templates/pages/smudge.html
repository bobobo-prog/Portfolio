Art by Art-ificial Intelligence

One of the Applications of Generative Adverserial Networks (GANs) is generating art. The images below are of resolution 256x265 , generated by a DCGAN that was trained for around 4-5 hours , on works by popular artists like Albrecht Durer , Claude Monet , Frida Kahlo , Henri Mati, Vincent Van-Gogh,Da-Vinci and a ton of artists. Sadly Google Colab doesnt have unlimited resources for free , beggars cant be choosers :( . To me these images are pretty cool considering the fact that they were generated by code and I think there's a lot of room for improvement like making the image size larger and extending the training time . Any updates made to this project will be posted here.
